Professional AI Stance

Help preservice teachers move from general ethical awareness to a practical, revisable professional stance on AI use.

Start template

A stance should be

  • Student-centered
  • Privacy-aware
  • Transparent
  • Flexible and revisable

Opening

Teachers need commitments, boundaries, and habits.

Teachers do not need a final answer for every AI situation they will encounter. But they do need a professional stance: a set of commitments, boundaries, questions, and habits that guide decision-making as tools, policies, and student needs change.

Questions Before Trying a Tool

Keep asking

  1. What learning goal does this support?
  2. What data does it collect?
  3. Can students opt out?
  4. Is there a non-AI alternative?
  5. Who benefits and who may be harmed?
  6. What human review is needed?
  7. What policy applies?
  8. How will I know whether it helped?

Capstone Prompt

What kind of teacher do you want to be in relation to AI? Name one commitment, one boundary, one concern, one use you are open to exploring, and one question you will keep asking.
