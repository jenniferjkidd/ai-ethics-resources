These pathways help faculty pair a site activity with a case or discussion prompt and a deeper Canvas resource. Use them to plan a short discussion, a class activity, or a preservice-teacher assignment.

Planning Question

What do you want preservice teachers to practice: noticing, questioning, evaluating, deciding, or explaining?

How To Use

Each pathway has three layers.

Activity Layer

The tool, checklist, template, or module students can use directly.

Discussion Layer

A case or question that lets students practice judgment in context.

Canvas Layer

The deeper background resource you can share through Canvas when students need more explanation.

Adaptation Layer

A suggestion for using the pathway in teacher-preparation courses.

Pathway 01

Start Ethical Decision-Making

Use when preservice teachers need a shared framework for deciding whether an AI use is appropriate, not just possible.

Framework

Pair With

Pathway 02

Evaluate an AI Tool Before Use

Use when faculty want students to practice asking privacy, safety, approval, and accountability questions before classroom adoption.

Tool Review

Use This Activity

AI Tool Privacy Audit, AI Tool Safety Checklist, or School AI Policy Map

Pair With

Student AI Companion or Predictive Student-Risk System

Canvas Background

4.4 | Data Privacy and Surveillance and 4.5 | Is AI Training on My Data?

Teacher-Prep Move

Have students distinguish teacher-only uses from student-facing uses, then identify what approval or disclosure would be needed.
